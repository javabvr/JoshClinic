    About Us

    A specialized clinical Rehabilitation service dedicated for pain and movement recovery with focus on systematic assessment and individualized treatment approach through clinical and research experience.

    JOSH Clinic had its inception in May 2011 as a small time clinic started by Physiotherapist Joshua Samuel Rajkumar, specialized in Neurology from Dr MGR Medical University, Chennai. He then went on to pursue working in a Rehabiliation centre in the Clinical and Academic domains at Bangalore for 6 years before starting up the clinic again. JOSH Clinic is now rejuvenated on January 2020 at Bangalore, along with his wife Physiotherapist Divya Joshua who also graduated in Physiotherapy from Dr MGR Medical University, Chennai and worked in National Hospital & MV Centre for Diabetes in Chennai, to foresee our dream of delivering the best quality of rehabilitation services to individuals with various challenges.

    Vision: To provide the best possible, accessible and affordable rehabiliation services to individuals with various challenges.

    Mission: Empowering individuals with physical difficulties, the need for rehabiliation and helping then get the best out of themselves through a better understanding of their conditions and how their body functions.

    Values: State of the art (Innovation/Research/Recent Advances), Professionalism (Enhancing skills and proficiency, Trust worthy), Compassion (Empathising and a source of support for the patients), Philanthropy (patient care above eveything else and afforable services for individuals with various challenges)

    Why Choose “JOSH Clinic” for your REHABILITATION needs?

    • Comprehensive assessment
    • Phased protocol based individualized treatment
    • Patient-Caregiver Education for understanding the underlying conditions and treatment offered
    • Continuous monitoring with regular follow-up interim assessment to evaluate the progress
    • Availability of multi-disciplinary team services
    • Experienced treating professionals
    • Competitive pricing for the short or long term services offered
    • Successful recoveries and positive client feedbacks

    Mode of Rehabilitation

    Since COVID19 lockdown has begun, we have explored various modes of delivering our clinical services through Online, Clinic (on appointments) and Home Care (selective patients) based on the requirements and specific needs of the patients and goals.

    Conditions Treated

    Musculoskeletal and Sports Injuries - Arthritis, Tendinopathies, Strains & Sprains, Post traumatic musculoskeletal complications like dislocations and fractures, Joint Replacement, Spondylosis and related degenerative conditions, Myofascial Pain syndrome, Fibromyalgia, Neuropathic Pain, Spinal deformities like Scoliosis, Kyphosis &Lordosis, Headaches and Joint pains.


    Neurodevelopmental and Adult Neurological Conditions - Cerebral Palsy, Spina Bifida, Stroke, Parkinson Disease, Spinal Cord Injury, Vestibular Disorders


    Cardio-Pulmonary Conditions - COPD, Altered Breathing Patterns and related disorders


    Women’s Health - Pre&post Partum fitness, Diastasis Recti, Endometriosis, Pelvic floor dysfunction


    Occupational Related Disorders - Work related musculoskeletal disorders / Repetitive strain injuries
